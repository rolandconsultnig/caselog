## 🌍 Nigerian SGBV Laws Covered

### Violence Against Persons Prohibition Act (VAPPA)
- Section 1: Rape (Life imprisonment)
- Section 6: FGM (4 years + fine)
- Section 9: Forceful ejection (2 years + fine)
- Section 13: Forced isolation (6 months + fine)
- Section 14: Emotional abuse (1 year + fine)
- Section 15: Harmful widowhood practices (2 years + fine)
- Section 16: Abandonment (3 years + fine)
- Section 19: Spousal battery (3 years + fine)
- Section 23: Incest (5-10 years)
- Section 25: Incest (with/without consent)

### Child's Rights Act (CRA)
- Section 23(d): Child marriage (₦500,000 + 5 years)
- Section 24: Tattoos on child (₦5,000 + 1 month)
- Section 25: Drug exposure (Life imprisonment)
- Section 30: Child trafficking (10 years)
- Section 31: Sexual intercourse with child (Life imprisonment)

### Cybercrime Act (CCA)
- Section 23: Child pornography (₦10-20M + 5-10 years)
- Section 28: Drugging for sexual purposes (₦5M + 10 years)

### Penal Code
- Section 237: Child abandonment (7 years + fine)
- Section 281: Trafficking in women (7 years + fine)
- Section 283: Rape (Life imprisonment + fine)
- Section 390: Incest with consent (7 years + fine)

### Trafficking in Persons Act (TPPA)
- Section 13: Trafficking (2+ years + fine)
- Section 14: Importation/exportation (5+ years + fine)
- Section 15: Procurement for sexual exploitation (5 years + fine)
