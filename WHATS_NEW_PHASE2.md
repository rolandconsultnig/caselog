## 1️⃣ Evidence Chain of Custody 🔗

### What It Does
Tracks every movement of evidence from collection to court presentation, ensuring legal integrity and admissibility.

### Key Features
- ✨ **Complete Transfer History** - Every transfer is logged with full details
- ✨ **Custodian Tracking** - Know who has custody at all times
- ✨ **Tamper Detection** - Seal integrity and tamper evidence tracking
- ✨ **Digital Signatures** - Secure signature collection for transfers
- ✨ **Legal Compliance** - Automatic compliance verification
- ✨ **Condition Monitoring** - Track evidence condition throughout custody

### How It Works
```
Evidence Collected → Chain Created → Transfers Logged → Court Presentation
```

### Example Use Case
```
1. Officer collects DNA sample
2. Chain of custody created
3. Sample transferred to forensic lab (logged)
4. Lab completes analysis
5. Sample transferred to evidence room (logged)
6. Sample presented in court (logged)
7. Complete audit trail available for legal proceedings
```

### Benefits
- ✅ Legal admissibility ensured
- ✅ Complete audit trail
- ✅ Tamper detection
- ✅ Compliance verification
- ✅ Court-ready documentation

- ✨ **Credibility Assessment** - Rate and track witness reliability
- ✨ **Protection Measures** - Track threats and implement protection
- ✨ **Testimony Scheduling** - Court appearance management
- ✨ **Risk Assessment** - Identify and mitigate risks

### Witness Types
1. **Eyewitness** - Directly witnessed the incident
2. **Character Witness** - Testifies about character

### Protection Levels

### Example Use Case

2. Statement recorded (audio + text)
3. Credibility assessed as "High"

5. Protection measures implemented

### Benefits

- ✅ Court preparation

## 3️⃣ Legal Charge Tracking ⚖️

### What It Does
Tracks legal charges from filing through trial, verdict, sentencing, and appeals.

### Key Features
- ✨ **Multiple Charges** - Track multiple charges per case
- ✨ **Evidence Linking** - Link evidence to specific charges
- ✨ **Witness Linking** - Link witnesses to charges
- ✨ **Plea Tracking** - Record pleas and plea bargains
- ✨ **Trial Management** - Schedule and track trials
- ✨ **Verdict Recording** - Document verdicts and details
- ✨ **Sentence Management** - Track sentences and appeals

### Charge Lifecycle
```
Charge Filed → Plea Entered → Trial Held → Verdict → Sentence → Appeal (if any)
```

### Plea Types
- **Guilty** - Admits guilt
- **Not Guilty** - Denies guilt
- **No Contest** - Does not contest
- **Not Entered** - Plea not yet entered

### Verdict Types
- **Guilty** - Found guilty
- **Not Guilty** - Found not guilty
- **Dismissed** - Case dismissed
- **Acquitted** - Acquitted of charges

### Sentence Types
- **Custodial** - Prison sentence
- **Non-Custodial** - No prison time
- **Fine** - Monetary fine
- **Probation** - Probation period
- **Community Service** - Community service
- **Suspended** - Suspended sentence

### Example Use Case
```
1. Charge filed: "Rape - Section 283 Penal Code"
2. Evidence linked: DNA sample, medical report
3. Witnesses linked: Victim, eyewitness
4. Plea entered: "Not Guilty"
5. Trial scheduled and held
6. Verdict: "Guilty"
7. Sentence: "Life imprisonment"
8. Complete legal record maintained
```

### Benefits
- ✅ Complete legal tracking
- ✅ Evidence-charge linking
- ✅ Trial preparation
- ✅ Verdict documentation
- ✅ Appeal management

## 5️⃣ Custody Transfer System 📦

### What It Does
Logs every transfer of evidence with complete details, signatures, and condition assessment.

### Key Features
- ✨ **Transfer Details** - From, to, date, time, location
- ✨ **Transfer Reasons** - Analysis, storage, court, return, disposal
- ✨ **Condition Assessment** - Before and after condition
- ✨ **Signatures** - Transferrer, receiver, witness
- ✨ **Verification** - Discrepancy tracking
- ✨ **Compliance** - Protocol compliance verification

### Transfer Reasons
1. **Analysis** - Forensic analysis
2. **Storage** - Long-term storage
3. **Court Presentation** - Present in court
4. **Return** - Return to custodian
5. **Disposal** - Authorized disposal
6. **Other** - Other reasons

### Condition Levels
- **Excellent** - Perfect condition
- **Good** - Good condition
- **Fair** - Fair condition
- **Poor** - Poor condition

### Example Use Case
```
1. Evidence transferred from officer to lab
2. Transfer details logged:
   - From: Officer Smith
   - To: Lab Tech Johnson
   - Reason: DNA Analysis
   - Condition Before: Good
   - Condition After: Good
3. Signatures collected
4. No discrepancies noted
5. Transfer complete and logged
```

### Benefits
- ✅ Complete transfer history
- ✅ Condition tracking
- ✅ Signature verification
- ✅ Discrepancy detection
- ✅ Legal compliance
